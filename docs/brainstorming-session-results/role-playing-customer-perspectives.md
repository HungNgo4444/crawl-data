# Role Playing - Customer Perspectives

## Customer Segment 1: Individual Quantitative Traders
**Profile**: Hedge fund quant managing $50M portfolio
**Requirements**:
- **Data Format**: JSON + CSV + API (flexibility required)
- **Latency**: 1-minute acceptable
- **Pricing**: $100-1,000/month (revised from initial $1,000)
- **Features**: Pre-processed data, asset-related entities, sentiment analysis, topic analysis, AI summaries

## Customer Segment 2: Enterprise Risk Management
**Profile**: Risk manager at large bank
**Requirements**:
- **Scale**: Global portfolio risk monitoring (credit, operational, reputational)
- **Compliance**: Mandatory audit trails and data lineage for regulatory reporting
- **Integration**: Seamless API integration with core banking systems (portfolio management, GRC)
- **Pricing**: $10,000+ monthly (complex pricing model based on users, data sources, support level)
- **Additional Concerns**: Legal liability, high-level data security, team collaboration features, vendor financial stability

## Customer Segment 3: Small Investment Advisory Firms  
**Profile**: Founder of $5M AUM advisory firm, 10 clients
**Requirements**:
- **Budget**: $20/month maximum (revised from $100-500)
- **Essential Features**: Real-time watchlists & alerts, reliable source coverage, clean focused interface, efficient search & filtering
- **User Experience**: Self-serve signup, credit card payment, 15-minute setup, mobile-friendly
- **Value Proposition**: Democratize access to professional tools, eliminate delay and noise from free sources
