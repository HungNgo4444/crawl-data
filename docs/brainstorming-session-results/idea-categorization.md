# Idea Categorization

## Immediate Opportunities (Ready to Implement)
1. **Vietnam News Crawler MVP** - Start with local sources (VnExpress, Dantri, etc.)
2. **Three-tier Pricing Structure** - Small firms ($20), Individual quant ($100-1000), Enterprise ($10K+)
3. **Raw Data Collection Strategy** - Build comprehensive database first, processing later
4. **Self-serve SaaS Model** - Credit card signup, 15-minute setup for small customers

## Future Innovations (Requires Development/Research)
1. **Domain Expertise Processing Engine** - Level 5 financial context understanding
2. **Multi-source Data Fusion** - News + economic indicators + social media + price movements
3. **Earnings Call Real-time Transcription** - Premium data source differentiation
4. **Government Filing Integration** - SEC, regulatory filings as alternative data source

## Moonshots (Ambitious, Transformative Concepts)
1. **Global Crypto Market Domination** - 24/7 borderless trading data service
2. **Financial Intelligence Platform** - Beyond news to comprehensive market intelligence
3. **$200M Annual Revenue** - Mature multi-tier global customer base
4. **AI-Powered Market Prediction** - Advanced correlation analysis across data types

## Insights & Learnings (Key Realizations)
1. **Processing Quality Over Data Quantity** - Competitive advantage lies in sophisticated analysis, not just data collection
2. **Customer Segmentation Critical** - Completely different needs, budgets, and features across segments  
3. **Vietnam-First Strategy** - Logical starting point for legal, linguistic, and market advantages
4. **Crypto as Global Catalyst** - Natural expansion path for worldwide market access
