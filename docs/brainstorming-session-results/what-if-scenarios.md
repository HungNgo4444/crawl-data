## Geographic Expansion Strategy
**Vietnam-First Approach**:
- Start with Vietnam market (easier legal compliance, local market understanding)
- Leverage Vietnamese language competitive advantage
- Lower initial infrastructure costs
- Test product-market fit before global expansion

**Global Expansion via Crypto**:
- **Crypto as catalyst**: Borderless 24/7 market, global audience immediately
- **Less regulatory barriers** compared to traditional finance
- **Higher volatility** = more trading decisions = higher willingness to pay
- **Tech-savvy user base** comfortable with digital tools
